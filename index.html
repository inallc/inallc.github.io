WHAT DO WE OFFER?

Ina LLC is formed by an expert team trained to develop games and apps for any kind of platform.

We offer a full range of develope including art for 2D and 3D projects, coding and programming, game design and audio.

We can offer you the perfect solution that meets the specific needs of your project.

 

 

 

WHY WORK WITH US?

Our philosophy is based on trust with the client. We will listen to your proposal and offer you the best solution for your project.

·Improving your game in every way.

·Contacting directly with the team.

·Flexibility and adaptability to your needs.

·Doing more in less time with the best results.

 

 

 

THE WAY WE WORK

Our professionals are a team of skilled and passionate game developers.

We always face the projects thinking of obtaining the highest quality. We offer forthright communication and collaboration to help you to accomplish your goals.

We can cooperate with your team or offer you a full solution to your ideas. Always with a relationship based on commitement and trust.
